MISSION STATEMENT

Most children's books fail to be ethnically diverse or sensitive to the specific needs of sick kids.
Mini Masterpieces is a children's book innovator, dedicated to creating customizable books to aid children who are, or have a loved one suffering from illnesses and disease. We strive to bridge the gap between children's books and the lack of representation by providing the ability to individualize and diversify the books to your own circumstances. The personal touch, and extra bit of love makes a world of a difference to a child.
